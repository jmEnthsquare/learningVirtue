Why Learn Web Development

95% of the industry is working on Linux platform. In your daily life, you are communicating with Linux servers, major internet sites such as Facebook, Flipkart, Naukri, Google and many more portal or deployed on Linux servers. In addition, most modern televisions and Android mobiles run on Linux and industry is looking for skilled resources to maintain the Linux servers.

On completion of this course you be eligible to apply for the following job roles Linux Administrator or Systems Engineer or Technical support expert or Application support Engineer or Sr.system Engineer and your duties will be to perform day to day administration activates over Linux platform.

The purpose of this training is to explore Linux administration. This training involves performing some of the day-to- day activities you would need to do as a Linux administrator such as creating a user, group, setting permissions for them, backing up data, restricting access to specific users, copying/moving files, managing processes and services, and securing system.
