TTT for soft skills Trainers

These workshops are a gateway for our new trainers to create contacts and join the L&D community. TTT in Scenario Based Learning

Since this is a unique training, we bring together our in house trainers who have been trained and certified in Scenario based learning. This is a one day workshop. This forum is very important for newly trained candidates as it enhances learning and also gives a forum to practice the methodologies learned in this program. This workshop will follow up with handing of a project which needs to be completed using these methodologies. A high quality but time sensitive workshop and project will ensure maximum learning retention and ensure application in day to day training sessions.


Technical/ IT Workshop


1. Workshop on Angular JS

This is a 3 days’ workshop for the web developer who want to enhance their technical skills for their career growth and challenge the competitive world with the latest technologies.
Who can attend?

  • Candidates who have knowledge on HTML5, CSS3, Java script.
  • Who have 1+ year of exp as a web developer.

Highlights of this program:

  • You will gain in-depth knowledge and will help you implement one case study using Angular JS
  • You will get clear understanding of using Angular JS over other Scripting languages.
  • Trainer would deliver this program using real time scenario’s
  • Gain the practical experience needed to build a solid, scalable, single page application with AngularJS.

2. Workshop on MongoDB.

Want to enhance your MongoDB World learning experience? Get hands-on practice by attending MongoDB workshops the day before MongoDB World. These in-person workshops provide the opportunity to receive comprehensive training and learn from MongoDB experts.


Duration: 3 days

Who can attend :

  • Any professional who has working knowledge of Database concepts.
  • Operation managers

Highlights of this program :

  • Expand your MongoDB knowledge with Advanced Data Modeling
  • Help you in Diagnostics and Debugging, or Modeling Social Data workshop.
  • Gain knowledge on collection & its implementation
  • Understand NoSql and its advantages
