TTT Soft-Skills



Description

The T3 program for soft skills is one of the most important commodity in any given field. You may be working in a BPO or an advertising firm or you must be a professional like a lawyer, doctor etc. – Soft skills is a rare skill as not many have it or even realize the absence of it, but be rest assured – others do.

Our 3 Day T3 program covers the following key skills:


  • Rapport development techniques in a multifaceted crowd
  • Listening skills
  • Identifying the questions and setting the objectives
  • Feedback – Praise and criticism
  • Psychological knowledge and reading the mind of the trainees
  • Motivational methods and self-evaluation
  • Team analysis and effective group development techniques
  • People's problem – (Stress, conflict), identifying and solving
  • Handling question and difficult people
  • Creating a conducive training environment, using humour, fun and delivery

The program overall takes care of four main verticals of training:

  • The Trainer – Methods, techniques and style
  • The Training Model – Designing and developing
  • The Program -- Critical factors, development and delivery
  • The Audience -- Engaging, rapport development and understanding

The Training courses are developed with a perfect structure of curriculum. In addition to this, the Train the Trainer course is affordable and is thus in range among prospective trainers around the world. This trainer training program is ideal for even professional trainers aspiring to get a training certification but run at a short time.
