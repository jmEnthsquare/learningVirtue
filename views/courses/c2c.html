College to Corporate



Description

The purpose of this program is to prepare the Fresh graduates to step to the next stages, i.e. Group Discussion (GD) and Interview, classes are conducted in Communication Skills which include basic communication skills, GD and Interview basics, mock GDs, mock Interviews (HR & Technical) and Psychometric tests.

Duration: 10 working days

Fees: Rs - 5,000
